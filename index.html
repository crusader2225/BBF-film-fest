ABOUT THE VENUE

Brooklyn Bridge Park spans 85 acres on the Brooklyn side of the East River of New York City. The park is overseen by a non-profit organization by the name of Brooklyn Bridge Park Corporation. The organization's mission is to "create and maintain a world class park that is a recreational, environmental and cultural destination enjoyed by residents of, and visitors to, New York City".
